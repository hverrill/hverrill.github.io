Just Enough Benadryl

Humans have told stories for as long as we could speak. Oral Storytelling was, and still is, one of the primary ways we pass on knowledge to our kin. In a digital storytelling class, I was assigned to tell a story orally. I was originally was going to create a folk tale from scratch, but couldn't think of anything I found compelling. Then, I remembered the assignment was not limited to real experiences. So I chose to write about something that happened in my life that others might find interesting or funny, while also sharing my experience with a chronic disease. Enjoy.
