FIRST Robotics

Throughout high school, I was a member of 3 different FIRST robotics teams. Though FIRST is a high school extracurricular, it is responsible for many of the engineering and professional communication skills I know today. I was the founder and co-captain of a FIRST FTC team and the lead programmer on each team. Successful FTC teams are run like start-up companies, with their own business plan and a marketing and outreach plan. The team creates documents on product development and presents its work to a panel of judges. The robot is only a small part of the challenge.
